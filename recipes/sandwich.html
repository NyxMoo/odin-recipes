Sandwich :)

Description

Some fresh toast with ham and cheese on top.

If you're feeling adventurous, you could put another slice of toast on top of that!

Ingredients

  • Toast
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Ham slices
  • (And optionally)
    • Pickles
    • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. (Optional but recommended) Put toast in toaster
  2. Make sure toast is golden brown and put butter on one side
  3. Place a slice of ham on toast
  4. Place a slice or two of cheese on top of the ham
  5. Place other optional ingredients on top
  6. Finish with other toast slice or eat as is
